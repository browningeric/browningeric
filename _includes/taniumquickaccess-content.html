Tanium Quick Access

Supercharging user navigation.

The challenge

A feature of the Tanium interface allowed users to place quick links to specific views on various overview pages. These were not being used at a level we wanted though, and I was tasked with finding a way to increase adoption without requiring extensive back-end resources to build.

Meeting Users Preferences

Through research I knew that a high percentage of our users were keyboard users, but we offered them little in the platform outside of basic accessibility and functionality.

I started exploring keyboard shortcut ideas and realized this was a perfect fit to access the quick links functionality. Instead of being limited to certain views, a user could use a keyboard shortcut to search from a list of available quick links.
